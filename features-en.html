⚡ Quick decision

Quick Analysis — 30-second verdict

Module #01 · Balanced tier · ~$0.005-0.02/analysis

Type a ticker (NVDA, BTC, "European luxury market") and the AI gives you in 30 seconds a BUY / SELL / HOLD verdict with a 0-100 score and a 5-axis breakdown: valuation, growth, risk, sentiment, diversification (portfolio mode). Ideal for the first reflex before digging deeper or to quickly validate intuition.

Watchlist + Daily Brief

Module #02 · Balanced tier · web search included

Add tickers to your watchlist and the app generates each morning a brief of 24h news, price moves, and important signals on your positions. Native web search via Perplexity or Grok for ultra-fresh data. Perfect to stay informed without scrolling Twitter.

📊 Fundamental analysis

Research Agent — full multi-angle analysis

Module #03 · Flagship tier · ~$0.30-1.00/analysis

The most exhaustive module. Provide a ticker, the AI agent produces ~6-12K tokens of analysis covering fundamentals, valuation, risks, catalysts, sector comparables, competitive position. Web search optional for fresh data. Structured output to serve as a basis for an investment note.

10-K Decoder — Buffett-style value analysis

Module #04 · Flagship tier · native PDF Claude/Gemini

Drop the annual report (10-K, 20-F, French URD reference document) and the AI extracts: moat (durable competitive advantage), ROIC, management quality, capital allocation signals, accounting red flags, dilution. Buffett format: you seek "wonderful companies at fair prices." Run once a year at earnings publication.

DCF Calculator — intrinsic value

Module #05 · Balanced tier

Complete Discounted Cash Flow in 30 seconds. Enter current FCF, growth Y1-5 / Y6-10, terminal growth, WACC, net debt, diluted shares. The AI produces a 10-year projection, Gordon terminal value, 3×3 sensitivity (WACC ± 1% × Growth ± 1%), margin of safety, UNDERVALUED / FAIR / OVERVALUED verdict. To validate a long-term high-conviction buy.

Investment Memo — structured institutional memo

Module #06 · Flagship tier

Provide bull case (3-5 reasons), bear case, 12-month catalysts, financial data. The AI writes a hedge-fund-style structured memo: thesis statement, risk/reward, position sizing recommendation, exit triggers. To formalize a thesis before a high-conviction buy, or share it (IRR, investment club, team).

Pre-Mortem — devil's advocate

Module #07 · Flagship tier

Describe your investment thesis in detail. The AI "imagines you've lost money" and gives you the 5 most likely reasons for failure. Charlie-Munger-style perspective inversion. Use before a major commitment, or when your conviction feels "too obvious."

Stock Screener — fundamental discovery

Module #08 · Balanced tier · optional web search

Define your criteria (P/E < X, ROIC > Y, debt < Z, sector, market). The AI scrolls its corpus + the web to propose 10-15 exact candidates with quantified rationale for each. Markets: US, EU, FR, UK. Styles: value, quality, growth, dividend, deep value.

Portfolio Audit — Buffett-style audit

Module #09 · Flagship tier

Deep audit of an entire portfolio (imported from Wealth or pasted manually). 8 sections: concentration, valuation, narrative fatigue, red flags, conviction × potential matrix, decorrelation, action recommendations, global 0-100 score. Distinct from the Rebalancer (tactical) — this is honest fundamental criticism, "letter to a smart investor" style.

Trade Journal — pattern & bias detection

Module #10 · Balanced tier

Paste your past trades (CSV or paragraphs). The AI detects recurring patterns: you sell winners too early, double down on losers, your real edge is sectoral, your volatility increases in the morning… Actionable insights to improve as a trader/investor. Refresh quarterly.

🌍 Macro & risk

Macro Dashboard — current regime and scenarios

Module #11 · Flagship tier · auto web search

Auto mode with live web search, or manual mode where you provide your inputs. Synthesis of the current macro regime (rates, inflation, growth, dollar, yield curve, HY OAS) + 3 scenarios at 3-12 months (base, bull, bear) with expected impact on asset classes. Run at the start of each month or on each important shift (FOMC, CPI, election).

Stress Test — 6 extreme macro regimes

Module #12 · Flagship tier

Describe your portfolio (1 line per asset). The AI simulates the expected impact across 6 extreme scenarios: 2008-style recession, 70s stagflation, COVID crash, hyperinflation, major war, 1970s rate shock. Output: estimated drawdown per scenario, contributions per position, hedge recommendations.

Portfolio Rebalancer — target allocation

Module #13 · Balanced tier

Provide your current positions + risk profile (1-5) + conviction level. The AI computes the target allocation and proposes a list of risk-adjusted trades to get there. Use 1-2× per year or after a major market move.

Battle Mode — 2-asset 5-round debate

Module #14 · Balanced tier · web search

NVDA vs AMD? Bitcoin vs Ethereum? Apple vs Microsoft? The AI plays both sides in 5 structured rounds: valuation, growth, risks, catalysts, final verdict. When you hesitate between 2 candidates for the same thesis.

🪙 Crypto

Crypto Fundamental — tokenomics and valuation

Module #15 · Balanced tier · auto-fetch CoinGecko

Type the symbol (ETH, SOL, AVAX). Auto-fetch CoinGecko for market data. The AI produces: tokenomics (supply, inflation, upcoming unlocks), annualized revenue / fees, P/F ratio (DeFi P/E equivalent), sector comparables, narrative and structural risks. Before buying a crypto, distinguish serious project from hype.

Whitepaper Reader — anti-scam scan

Module #16 · Balanced tier · native PDF

Drop the whitepaper PDF. The AI detects classic scam patterns: toxic tokenomics (excessive team allocation), anonymous team, unrealistic claims (projected TVL vs chain, promised ROI), copy-paste of other projects, lack of real tech. A must before ANY purchase of a new or little-known project.

📡 Sentiment

Sentiment Tracker — contrarian signals

Module #17 · Balanced tier · web search

The AI scans Reddit, X, financial news, specialized forums on a ticker, classifies sentiment (bullish/bearish/mixed), identifies divergences with fundamentals. The "sentiment-vs-fundamentals gap" is one of the best known contrarian signals. Use when you hesitate on entry/exit timing.

Earnings Call Forensics — transcript decoding

Module #18 · Flagship tier · long context (Claude/Gemini)

Paste the transcript or drop the PDF. The AI detects weak signals CEOs try to hide: tone shifts vs last quarter, sandbagging, question dodging, rising caution words. Q-1 notes accepted for cross-comparison. Run each quarter on your active positions.

YouTube Transcript + CEO Forensics

Module #19 · Flagship tier · cross-module memory

Decode a YouTube transcript (earnings call, podcast, conference) with deep linguistic analysis: quantified verbal sentiment (% confidence vs caution words), avoided topics, repeated narratives, 0-100 confidence score. The generated memory is reinjectable into Sentiment Tracker, 10-K Decoder, etc. for a cross-source view.

Newsletter Voice — newsletter in your style

Module #20 · Flagship tier · style cloning

Step 1: train the style clone by pasting 3-5 past texts you wrote. Step 2: provide key bullet points and the AI generates a complete newsletter in your voice. For publishers: save 80% of writing time without diluting your personal tone.

🛠 Tools

Position Sizing — Kelly Criterion

Module #21 · Fast tier · ~$0.01/analysis

Provide conviction (1-10), correlation with existing positions, desired risk cap. The AI computes the optimal position size via Kelly Criterion (fraction = (bp - q) / b) adjusted for correlation and volatility. Before each trade to avoid oversizing from excessive conviction.

FIRE Calculator — financial independence date

Module #22 · Balanced tier · French tax context

Provide income, expenses, allocation, growth projection. The AI computes your FIRE (Financial Independence Retire Early) date with French tax nuances (PEA, AV, PER). Ideal for anyone following the FIRE movement.

Voice — audio in/out

Web Speech API · 100% client-side

Voice input to dictate long prompts (thesis analyses, voice brief from an earnings call listened to while walking). Voice output to listen to the generated newsletter. No external API — uses the browser's Web Speech API.

Advanced French tax strategy: PEA / Assurance-Vie / PER optimization, deductions, gifting, FCPI/FIP, deferrable deficit, fiscal relocation. Provide marginal tax bracket, savings capacity, wealth situation. Disclaimer: educational analysis, certified tax advisor validation recommended > €10K.

Tax Optimizer International — 8 countries

Module #24 · Flagship tier

USA (cap gains, IRA, 401k), UK (ISA, capital gains), Belgium, Switzerland (cantonal), Spain, Germany, Italy, Portugal. Each country has its specifics: the AI knows the regimes, allowances, deadlines, bilateral tax treaties. For expats, dual residents, or before fiscal relocation.

💼 Personal finance (V7-V12)

Monthly budget

Local module · IndexedDB

Enter income / fixed / variable expenses / savings. Computes savings rate and investment capacity, projects 30 years at 7% return. Connected to bank CSV (12 pre-mapped FR banks) for auto-import.

Hidden fees

LLM-augmented module · 100 European ETF database

Detects TER, entry fees, AV management, brokerage. Compares with cheaper alternatives from the embedded base. Computes compounded impact over 10/20/30 years: a 0.85% AV vs 0.30% ETF = -€80K over 30 years.

Dividend tracker

Local module · top 80 CAC 40 + SBF 120 embedded

Monthly calendar, yield on cost, annual projection, 5-year growth, passive-life coverage (how many months your dividends pay your lifestyle).

Diversification score 0-100

Local module · HHI + sector + geo + asset class algorithm

Objective measure: 40 pts concentration + 20 sectors + 20 geography + 20 asset classes. Detects tech over-concentration, lack of defensives, limited geo exposure. Strengths / weaknesses / recommendations.

French wealth method

Rules-engine module + optional LLM · 30 rules

Exhaustive FR checklist: switch bank AV → online, PEA/PER optimization, dismemberment, gift-sharing, flat tax. Saving potential computed for each rule. "Deepen with AI" button for personalized recommendation.

Bank CSV import

Local module · 12 pre-mapped FR banks

4-step wizard: bank selection → upload → mapping & preview → import. Auto-categorization from 50+ keywords. Boursorama, CA, BNP, LCL, SG, CM, BP, CE, ING, Hello bank, Trade Republic, Fortuneo + generic format.

Weekly auto-insights

Rules-engine module · 30+ rules · optional LLM

Generates 3-5 insights per week: concentration alert, €100K milestone, excessive fees, tax opportunity. Weekly throttle, dismissable. 1-2 enriched insights with AI synthesis.

Price alerts (YouTube + manual)

Local module · regex extraction · Web Notifications

Automatically extracts price levels mentioned in YouTube transcripts ("AAPL above 220 → buy"). Shows a global red indicator when a level is hit. Manual entry also possible.

French IFI Simulator

Local module · IFI 2024 brackets

Computes the French wealth tax (Impôt sur la Fortune Immobilière) on your real estate after primary residence allowance (-30%) and deductible debts. Reduction strategies (dismemberment, charitable gifts -75%).

Goals — Financial targets

Local module · IndexedDB · wealth connection

Define your targets (€1M net at 50, FIRE at 45, €50K house down payment). Auto-reads wealth, computes progression, projects if trajectory is respected. Notification if gap > 25%.

Live Watcher

Real-time charts on your favorite tickers, refresh 30s, integrated price alerts. 1D/1W/1M selector.

Account view

Local module

Wealth grouped by tax wrapper: PEA, AV, PER, CTO, Livret A, cash. Tax view + savings cap per wrapper.

Wealth projection

🎁 Advanced V11-V12 — 10 new modules

Gifts & Inheritance FR

Local module · CGI 2026 brackets

Exhaustive simulator: €100K/child every 15 years allowance, €152,500/beneficiary AV before 70, temporary dismemberment (bare-ownership gift), direct line and siblings brackets. Includes children's inheritance + spouse exempt. The module that alone justifies the app for wealth > €500K.

Capital Gains Tracker (FIFO/LIFO/WAC)

Local module · per-lot tracking

Tracks each purchase lot (date + qty + cost). Compares taxation across FIFO / LIFO / WAC before each sale, with PFU 30% calculation. Auto-import from Wealth. Massive FR retail gap: neither Boursorama nor IBKR do it well.

Backtest 1990-2025

Local module · embedded historical data

"How much would I have today if I had invested €200/month in MSCI World since 2010?" Simulates DCA or lump-sum on 6 typical portfolios (S&P 500, MSCI World, CAC 40 GR, 60/40, 80/20, World+BTC). Computes CAGR, multiple, max drawdown, strategy comparison.

Multi-currency P&L

Local module · auto currency detection

Separates spot performance from FX impact. Ex: your S&P 500 made +12% in USD, but USD dropped 3% vs EUR → +9% net in EUR. Critical for those holding USD (>30% of portfolio).

Earnings Calendar

Local module · filtered on your positions

Lists upcoming earnings only for stocks/ETFs you hold. Day countdown, dates inferred by typical quarterly cycle. Manual override per ticker.

Correlation Matrix

Local module · MSCI/BlackRock long-term averages

Heatmap of correlations across 7 asset classes + weighted average correlation of YOUR portfolio. A score > 0.7 = highly correlated (poorly diversified); < 0.4 = well diversified.

ESG Impact Investing

Local module · MSCI ESG / Sustainalytics base

E/S/G score per holding, fossil exposure, controversies. Weighted portfolio score. SFDR mega-trend, especially for investors < 40.

Estate Documents FR (Generator)

Local module · 3 legal templates

Generates draft: (1) Holographic will (to recopy by hand, art. 970), (2) Future protection mandate (art. 477), (3) Letter of instructions to notary with all your assets. .txt download. 70% of French people die without a will.

Macro Events Calendar

Local module · 90 days

Fed/ECB/CPI/NFP calendar with expected impact (high/medium/low). All known 2026 dates + custom event entry (elections, summits). Weekly Monday review.

Performance Attribution (Brinson)

Local module · MSCI World benchmark

Decomposes your alpha into 2 sources: (1) allocation = good sector weights, (2) selection = good stocks within each sector. Lets you know if your outperformance comes from luck or skill.
