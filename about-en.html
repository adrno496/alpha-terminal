About Alpha Terminal

The AI financial analysis terminal designed for retail investors, built in France.

Who we are

Alpha Terminal is a web app (with desktop, Android and iOS versions) combining 46 AI financial analysis modules — fundamental analysis, DCF valuation, 10-K and earnings call decoding, multi-country tax optimization, wealth tracking, FIRE projection — powered by 14 LLM providers of your choice (Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini, xAI Grok, Mistral, Cerebras, etc.).

We believe institutional-grade financial analysis, which cost €24,000/year at Bloomberg, should be accessible to every serious investor for €9.99/month — with absolute privacy guarantee: your data never leaves your browser.

Our mission

Democratize institutional financial analysis, without data collection, without single-vendor dependence, at AI cost.

Concretely, this means three principles:

  • BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) — you bring your own API key. The app calls LLMs directly from your browser. No Alpha intermediary server. You pay AI at the official rate, no markup.
  • 100% client-side — local AES-GCM-256 + PBKDF2 100k iter encryption for your key vault. All analyses, wealth, and notes stored in local IndexedDB. Verifiable in DevTools → Network: you only see calls to provider APIs.
  • Open architecture — 14 LLMs supported, 11 financial data providers, multi-country (US, UK, BE, CH, ES, DE, IT, PT, FR), bilingual FR/EN.
