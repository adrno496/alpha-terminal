BYOK: why it's the future of AI SaaS

BYOK means Bring Your Own Key. Instead of including AI costs in an inflated subscription (which you pay even when not using), Alpha lets you connect your own API key directly to the provider of your choice: Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini, xAI Grok, Mistral, Cerebras, GitHub Models, NVIDIA NIM, Hugging Face, Cloudflare Workers AI, Together AI, Cohere, OpenRouter, or Perplexity.

You pay at the official rate, only what you consume, without intermediary markup. For typical usage (5-10 analyses per day), monthly cost is $2 to $8 — compared to €30-200/month for traditional AI SaaS that take 5-10× the actual cost.

Pricing FAQ

How much does Alpha cost?

€9.99/month. You also pay AI providers for your usage (~$0.005-0.10/analysis). Cancel anytime.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 7-day free trial on the monthly plan. Plus, demo mode lets you explore all modules with pre-generated analyses without paying or connecting an API key.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, in 1 click from the app (Settings) or your App Store / Play Store account. No minimum commitment. Your data remains accessible locally even after cancellation.

How much do AI analyses cost?

Highly variable depending on the module and chosen provider. A Quick Analysis costs ~$0.005-0.02. A 10-K Decoder can go up to $1 depending on PDF size and model. Cerebras and GitHub Models offer near-free tiers for those who want to minimize costs. With Eco Mode enabled in Settings, save up to 70%.

Are there hidden fees?

None. The subscription covers the app + future modules + updates. The only extra cost is your AI usage at the providers you choose (BYOK = Bring Your Own Key). Alpha runs no servers, so no hidden infrastructure costs.

What if I lose my password?

Your API keys are encrypted with your local password (AES-GCM 256). Without the password, you can reset the vault and reconfigure — past analyses remain accessible.

Does the app work offline?

The app itself yes (PWA). But analysis requires connection to call the LLM APIs of providers you use.
